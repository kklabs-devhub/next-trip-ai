Privacy Policy

Emergency Info Card+

Effective date: April 2026

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data from users. The app may store information that you manually enter on your device, such as your name, date of birth, medical notes, blood group, allergies, and emergency contacts.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used only for displaying and managing your personal information within the app.

Data Storage

All information is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, sell, or share your personal information with any third parties.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or external tracking services.

Data Security

We aim to protect your information by keeping it stored locally on your device. However, you are responsible for securing access to your device.

Your Control Over Information

You can edit or delete your personal information at any time directly within the app.

Your Consent

By using this app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy

Contact
