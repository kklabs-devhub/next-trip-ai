Privacy Policy

AI Trip Planner

Effective Date: May 2026

AI Trip Planner respects your privacy. We are committed to protecting your personal information and providing transparency about how data is handled within the app.

Information We Collect

AI Trip Planner may store information that you voluntarily provide within the app, such as travel preferences, destinations, trip details, travel notes, itineraries, budgets, and saved travel plans.

Depending on app features you choose to use, the app may also process prompts or travel-related requests to generate AI-powered travel recommendations and itineraries.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used solely to provide and improve your travel planning experience, including:

  • Generating travel itineraries and recommendations
  • Saving and managing trips
  • Providing travel planning assistance
  • Personalizing app features and preferences

Data Storage

Trip information and app preferences may be stored locally on your device to provide a better user experience.

If AI-powered features are used, travel-related requests may be securely processed through third-party AI service providers to generate responses. We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

AI Trip Planner may use trusted third-party services to provide AI-generated travel planning functionality. These services may process travel-related prompts to generate recommendations.

The app currently does not use advertising or third-party tracking services.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and users are responsible for securing access to their devices.

Your Control Over Information

You can edit or delete your saved trips, travel information, and preferences directly within the app at any time.

Your Consent

By using AI Trip Planner, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support page listed on the App Store.
